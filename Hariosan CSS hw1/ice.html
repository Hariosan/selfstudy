Planet Nibiru

The concept of Nibiru was popularized by writer Zecharia Sitchin in his books, especially The 12th Planet (1976).

Sitchin claimed that ancient Sumerian texts described a distant planet named Nibiru.

According to his interpretation, Nibiru is part of our solar system but has an enormous, elliptical orbit that brings it close to Earth only once every 3,600 years.

Sitchin also theorized that Nibiru is home to a race of advanced extraterrestrial beings called the Anunnaki.

According to his writings, the Anunnaki supposedly came to Earth thousands of years ago, seeking gold and other resources, and they genetically engineered humans as a slave species to help them mine these resources. Although this theory has no basis in actual Sumerian texts, it became popular and inspired numerous conspiracy theories.
