The Other Side of the Moon

Some of the most popular theories claim that alien civilizations have hidden bases on the far side, out of Earth's sight, where they conduct activities that remain unknown to us. Others propose that secret human mining operations or even military bases could be stationed there, hidden from public view by powerful governments. Additionally, there are theories that NASA and other space agencies might have captured photographic evidence of strange structures on the far side, which has been deliberately kept classified.
